A veg burger is a sandwich made with a plant-based patty, usually consisting of vegetables, legumes, or grains, served in a bun with toppings like lettuce, tomato, onions, and sauces.


ingredients:

  • buns
  • veg patty
  • cheese slice
  • lettuce
  • tomato slices
  • onion rings
  • mayo
  • salt and pepper
  • butter

steps to follow:

  1. prepare the patty
  2. toast the buns
  3. assemble the burger
  4. serve
