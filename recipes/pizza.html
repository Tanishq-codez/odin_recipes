description

A pizza is a flatbread topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings like vegetables or herbs, baked to perfection with a crispy crust.


ingredients:

  • pizza base
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella cheese
  • capsicum
  • onions
  • olives
  • oregano
  • chili flakes

steps to follow:

  1. spread sauce on base
  2. add cheese and toppings
  3. bake until golden
  4. slice and serve
